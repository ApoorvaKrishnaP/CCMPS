# Metrics Explained

## 1. Density

Formula:

```text
Density = Total People / Area(m²)
```

Unit:

```text
people per square meter (p/m²)
```

---

## 2. Average Speed

Formula:

```text
Speed = Distance / Time
```

Unit:

```text
meters per second (m/s)
```

---

## 3. Inflow

People entering the region per minute.

Unit:

```text
people/min
```

---

## 4. Outflow

People leaving the region per minute.

Unit:

```text
people/min
```
